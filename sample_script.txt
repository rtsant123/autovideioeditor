A lone fisherman lives in a small coastal village. Every morning before sunrise he pushes his wooden boat into the cold sea.

One day a violent storm rolls in while he is far from shore. Waves crash over the boat as he fights to steer back home.

He barely survives, washing up on the beach at dawn, exhausted but alive. The village gathers around him.

That night, by the fire, he decides he will teach the young ones everything he knows about the sea, so no one faces it unprepared again.
